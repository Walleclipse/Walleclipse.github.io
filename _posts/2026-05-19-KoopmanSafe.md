We propose a data-driven framework that learns a Koopman embedding for nonlinear robot dynamics, integrating the resulting linear model with the Safe Set Algorithm to solve tracking and safety constraints in a single quadratic program. Validated on a Kinova Gen3 manipulator and a Go2 quadruped, achieving accurate tracking with obstacle avoidance.
