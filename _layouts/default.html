Abulikemu (Abu) Abuduweili

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Robotics Institute, at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Changliu Liu (Intelligent Control Lab). My research focuses on robot learning and control, human-robot collaboration, optimization, efficient machine learning, and computer vision.

I received my Bachelor’s degree and Master's degree in School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science at Peking University.

I am seeking a full-time research-oriented positions. If you think my expertise aligns with your needs, please feel free to reach out.
