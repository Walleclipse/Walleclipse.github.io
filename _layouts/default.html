Abulikemu Abuduweili

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Robotics Institute, at Carnegie Mellon University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Changliu Liu, in the Robotics Institute, School of Computer Science. Before joining CMU, I spent an enriching year as a research assistant with Prof. Humphrey Shi. I hold a Bachelor's and a Master's degree from the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science at Peking University. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Robotics, Embodied AI, and Computer Vision.

I gained industry experience through internships at Microsoft Research Redmond (with Dr. Lekan Molu and Dr. Naoki Wake), Toyota Research Institute (with Dr. Frank Permenter and Dr. Chenyang Yuan), Baidu Research (under Prof. Dejing Dou), and Microsoft Research Asia (with Dr. Qingwei Lin). Feel free to reach out for collaborations or to discuss research.

I am actively seeking a full-time, research-oriented position. If my expertise aligns with your needs, please feel free to reach out.

Research

I am broadly interested in the intersection of robotics and machine learning, with a particular focus on advancing lifelong, efficient, and robust learning and autonomy. Below are the topics I contribute to, along with references to related works. (Some papers cover multiple topics.)

  • Continual Learning: Memory-replay (T-RO 2023, CoRL 2023), Dynamic Architecture (arXiv 2024-1), Model Adaptation and Test-time Adaptation (CoRL 2023, ACSP 2021, L4DC 2020)
  • Data-efficient Learning: Semi/self-supervised Learning (PR 2023, CVPR 2021), Transfer Learning (PR 2023, CVPR 2021), Few-shot Learning (NeurIPS 2021)
  • Robot Learning: Human-robot Collaboration (arXiv 2024-2, CCTA 2023, AI-HRI 2019), Motion/Behavior Prediction (CoRL 2023, L4DC 2020, AI-HRI 2019), SLAM (T-RO 2023, arXiv 2022), Manipulation (CoRL 2024), Locomotion (arXiv 2024-1), Reinforcement Learning (TMLR 2023)
  • Robustness and Safety: Neural Network Robustness (CoRL Safe-ROL 2024), Certified Training (TMLR 2024), Safe Control (CCTA 2023)
  • Optimization: Adaptive Gradient Method (OPT 2024), Extended Kalman Filter (ACSP 2021, L4DC 2020)
  • Generative Models: Diffusion Models (arXiv 2024-3), GAN (T-RO 2023), LLM and RAG (medRxiv 2024), Encoder-Decoder (ACL-SRW 2019)
  • Neural Network Architecture: Transformer (arXiv 2021), Graph Neural Networks (NeurIPS 2021)
